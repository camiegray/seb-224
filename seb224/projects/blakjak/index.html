1. Each player is dealt two cards. You can see only one of the dealers cards until your turn is over.
  2. The goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  3. Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10, and Aces are worth 1 or 11.
  4. The player can choose to "Hit" to take another card or "Stand" to hold and let the dealer play.
  5. The dealer always hits when count is 16 or below.
  6. There are 6 decks in this game and each deck contains 4 copy of each cards
  7. In total, there are 24 copies of each cards totalling 312 cards
